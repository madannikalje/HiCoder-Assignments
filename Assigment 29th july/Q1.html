What are the major reasons behind client dissatisfaction in a web design project ? How can we avoid it ?

Reasons :-

1) Unsatisfactory design

2) Non-Responsive, Disappears

3) Technical Limitations

4) Slow to respond, Poor Service

5) Can’t find developer

6) Overpriced

How can we avoid it ?

1) Be responsive - When there’s an issue, resolve it immediately. By waiting to resolve an issue can turn smaller problems into bigger ones.

2) Be honest - Telling customers the truth usually goes over better than lying to them. It will eventually help gain the customers respect.

3) Be realistic - Some customers have expectations that are just aren’t attainable. In that case, it may be necessary to try to reset the customer’s expectations, or, if that isn’t possible, to suggest that they may be happier by taking their business elsewhere. Not every sale is worth the cost involved in obtaining it.
